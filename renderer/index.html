          ℹ️ Grant type acronyms
          

            
RIA
Research & Innovation Action — Collaborative research, TRL 1–4. Generates new knowledge; multiple partners required.

            
IA
Innovation Action — Close-to-market, TRL 5–8. Prototyping, pilots, demonstrations, first commercial application.

            
CSA
Coordination & Support Action — No primary research. Networking, dissemination, standardisation, policy support.

            
EIC
European Innovation Council — Pathfinder (early-stage), Transition (scale-up) and Accelerator (blended finance) for deep-tech startups & SMEs.

            
MSCA
Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions — Researcher mobility & training: Doctoral Networks (DN), Postdoctoral Fellowships (PF), Staff Exchanges (SE).

            
ERC
European Research Council — Frontier/basic research for individual PIs: Starting (StG), Consolidator (CoG), Advanced (AdG), Synergy (SyG).
